void EV_Crowbar ( event_args_t *  args)

Handles the crowbar swing event (miss).

This function plays a sound for a crowbar swing and triggers the corresponding weapon animation. It also determines if the swing was local (for the player's perspective) and plays the appropriate animation. The crowbar hit sound is played server-side, but the miss sounds are predicted client-side.
