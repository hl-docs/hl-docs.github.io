Conditionally spawns a tracer effect for a bullet.

Depending on the weapon type and a random chance, this function will display a tracer particle along the bullet trajectory. Tracers are typically shown for fast-firing automatic weapons like the MP5 or M249.

Parameters
idxIndex of the player or entity that fired the bullet.
vecSrcSource position of the bullet (typically muzzle or eye position).
vecEndEnd position of the bullet trajectory (hit point or max distance).
iBulletTypeType of bullet or weapon that fired the tracer.
iTracerCountNumber of bullets fired so far in this burst; used to control tracer frequency.

Fires multiple bullets from a weapon, handling spread, tracers, and decals.

This function simulates the firing of one or more bullets. It calculates bullet direction with spread, performs collision tracing, optionally generates tracer effects, and applies impact effects like decals and sounds depending on the surface hit.

Parameters
idxIndex of the entity firing the weapon.
forwardForward direction vector from player's view.
rightRight direction vector from player's view.
upUpward direction vector from player's view.
cShotsNumber of bullets to fire (e.g. shotgun uses >1).
vecSrcSource position of bullets (typically muzzle or eye position).
vecDirShootingDirection vector the bullets are intended to travel.
flDistanceMaximum distance the bullets can travel.
iBulletTypeType of bullet (e.g. 9mm, buckshot).
iTracerFreqHow often tracers should appear (e.g. every nth bullet).
tracerCountPointer to the tracer counter for this weapon.
flSpreadXHorizontal bullet spread.
flSpreadYVertical bullet spread.
