◆ UpdateBeams()

void UpdateBeams ( void  )

Updates the positions and lifetimes of the client-side beam effects.

This function traces a line from the player's view position forward into the world. It finds where that line hits something (like a wall or an enemy), and updates the beam effect so that it visually "connects" from the player to that hit location.

It works by:

  • Getting the player's view angles
  • Getting their last predicted position (so the beam appears correctly)
  • Performing a player trace (like a raycast) to find where the beam would hit
  • Updating the beam visuals to point to the hit location
