Inflicts damage to entities within a certain radius.

This function applies damage to all entities within a specified radius of an explosion or other damaging event, considering line of sight and water boundaries.

Parameters
vecSrcThe source position of the damage.
pevInflictorThe entity causing the damage.
pevAttackerThe entity responsible for the damage.
flDamageThe amount of damage to inflict.
flRadiusThe radius within which entities are affected.
iClassIgnoreThe class of entities to ignore.

Calculates the origin of a bounding model.

This function computes the origin point of a bounding model based on its minimum and size properties. The origin is determined as the center of the bounding box defined by the model's dimensions.
